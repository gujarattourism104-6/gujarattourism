About Botanical Garden :

Sneh Rashmi Botanical Garden is located on the bank of the Tapi river in Surat. Surat the industrial hub of Gujarat, also has a pleasant and green side to it. The city, which is also called Manchester of India is home to the Botanical Garden of India that has more than 1,000 plant species along with other attraction such as a toy train, Columbus, Injector, Free Fall Jump, Rocket Space, Crazy Jump and hot air balloon ride to enjoy the picturesque Surat.


If you are looking for a mesmerizing spot to spend the evening in Surat, look no further to the gorgeous Sneh Rashmi Botanical Garden. Bequeathing you with lovely contrasting hues of a host of variety of plants, trees and flowers along a stunning landscape, this place is just right to catcha glimpse of golden sunset, when the azure skies to tinges of tangerine! The clean, hygienic and very well-maintained environs are idle to enjoy a walk too. All in all, a very relaxing experience you can look forward to.


Address: Ugat - Bhesan Road, Ugat, Dahin Nagar, Surat, Gujarat 395005, India. Timings: 09:00 am - 08:00 pm . Ticket Price: 10 INR. Time Required: 01:30 Hrs
