About Gopi Talav :

Gopi Talav or Gopi Lake is a lake in the Gopipura locality in the city of Surat in Gujarat state of India. It was built in circa 1510 CE by Malik Gopi, who was an affluent merchant and governor of Surat during the Gujarat Sultanate.


Gopi Talav is one of the popular tourist destinations of Surat, This place looks much beautiful in the evenings. The lake has a fountain which showers water as a spring. The Lake has many fun activities like Boating, Bumper riding, Zipline rides. The lakes also have many kids zone where kids can play have fun. The place is also surrounded by fresh greenery and the water in the Talav is quite fascinating. This lake was constructed by Malik Gopi in 1510 CE.


Address: Rustampura, Surat, Gujarat 395002, India Timings: 10:00 am - 10:00 pm Ticket Price: 20 INR Time Required: 02:00 Hrs
