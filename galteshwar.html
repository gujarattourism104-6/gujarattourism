About Galteshwar Mahadev Temple:

the temple is a very pleasant place very peaceful place.The temple is on Naradi ganga, gupt ganga,tapi river sangam sthan place.The temple was built about 200 years old but it was renovated a few years ago. A huge idol of Load Shiva 62 ft height is the attraction of the place. At the Sangam special arrangement for a fiver, bathing is made which attracts people for hot days. Sangam Snan also they have 12 jyotirlinga temple in the complex, Shivalinga over here is Swayambhu.


the structure of this temple is carved on a red stone and it is eight-sided which was built during Solanki Era and the structure of the temple is one of the major attractions for the devotees and is the devotees who visit the temple rub their hands on the red stone that is the temple and keep that dust of the stone on their forehead which they believe as divine. Maha Shivratri is the main temple that is celebrated here and thousands of devotees visit the temple during the festival times to seek the blessings of the Lord.
